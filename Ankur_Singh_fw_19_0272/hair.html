The HairStore at SkinStore: What Your Routine Needs

Natalie Rosselli

Give your hair the same love and care you give your skin by shopping The HairStore at SkinStore. From treatments to tools, we’ve rounded up some of our favorite brands to help you discover the top products you need for your best hair day yet.

Phyto

Over 50 years ago, Patrik Alés, the founder of Phyto, set out to study plants and their power; how they’re used in traditional medicine and the potential role they could play in haircare. Phyto’s products are created with 95 percent natural formulas, utilizing over 700 different botanicals. Their lineup includes shampoo, conditioner, densifying and moisturizing treatments and more.

Phyto Phytojoba Moisturizing Mask 5.29 fl. oz
Phyto Phytocolor Color-Protecting Shampoo 8.45 fl. oz
Phyto Phytocyane Fortifying Densifying Shampoo 8.45 fl. oz

Raincry

Raincry is a luxury haircare brand that creates revolutionary pH balanced formulas that don’t alter color, highlights, perms or keratin treatments, making them perfect for daily use. It started when Raincry’s founder Feisal Qureshi was looking to replace his round brushes but was unsatisfied with what was on the market. He went to France to create innovative styling brushes that went on to be heralded within the beauty industry. Since then, Raincry has expanded its range to include shampoo and conditioner, hair fragrances, primers and leave-in treatments.

RAINCRY Rebuild Bond Repair 5.1oz
RAINCRY Condition Large Pure Natural Bristle Paddle Brush
RAINCRY Daily Densifying Treatment 5.1oz

Alterna

Founded in 1997, Alterna set out to create products that promoted sustainability without sacrificing luxury. The brand’s skin care–inspired formulas help to naturally build hair strength and radiance with unique ingredients such as caviar extract—and without parabens, SLS/SLES sulfates, synthetic color and other harsh chemicals.

Alterna Caviar Anti-Aging Replenishing Moisture Shampoo 16.5oz ($66)
Alterna Caviar Anti-Aging Replenishing Moisture Conditioner 16.5oz ($66)
Alterna CAVIAR Anti-Aging Replenishing Moisture Serum Capsules 20ml

ghd

A global leader in high-end professional performance styling tools, ghd works together with scientists and stylists to develop breakthrough haircare and styling technology. The brand creates award-winning tools, from flat irons to curling irons, that style at a scientifically proven optimum temperature of 365°F for long-lasting styles without extreme heat damage, keeping your hair shiny and healthy.

ghd Unplugged Cordless Styler - Black
ghd Original Styler Flat Iron iD Collection - Soft Pink
ghd Platinum+ Styler - White $279.00

Virtue

Virtue works to empower people through the health and appearance of their hair, with a mission of changing the way customers look and feel. The brand utilizes the benefits of Alpha Keratin 60ku® in its products, a protein that makes up 90 percent of our hair, skin and nails. This protein is naturally recognized by the body and is pulled to areas it’s most needed, helping to smooth, resurface and fill in cracks of damage in hair.

VIRTUE One for All 6-in-1 Styler Cream 120ml
VIRTUE Restorative Treatment Mask 150ml
VIRTUE Recovery Conditioner 200ml

Grow Gorgeous

Grow Gorgeous creates nutrient-rich products to help transform hair from the root. The brand has seven ranges that cater to diverse hair types, all of which come with the Grow Gorgeous Purely-Positive Promise: a lineup that’s vegan, cruelty-free and formulated without parabens, sulfates, silicones, phthalates and mineral oil. These products harness the power of amino acids, allantoin, caffeine, ceramides and hyaluronic acid to help deliver long-lasting results for healthy hair.

Grow Gorgeous Hair Density Serum Original
Grow Gorgeous Repair Strengthening Hair Scalp
Grow Gorgeous Prebiotic and AHA 5% Booster

Nioxin

Nioxin takes an innovative skin care approach to its haircare, and creates products that work to strengthen, nourish and treat the hair starting at your scalp. The brand believes in tailored solutions, understanding that everyone’s hair has different needs. Each 3-part system is designed to create the ideal foundation for fuller and thicker hair, formulated with a variety of hair types in mind.
