Which TriPollar Device is Right for You?

Natalie Rosselli

In 2008, TriPollar launched themselves into the home beauty sphere with an aim to innovate the technology behind at-home treatments and devices. They utilize two technologies that are safe and efficient, recreating the results of expensive spa treatments at home with the same professional results.

TriPollar ® RF (radio frequency) technology, showcased in the STOP X and STOP Vx devices, works to increase the production of collagen and elastin to help erase signs of aging. It’s clinically proven to heat both the superficial (dermal) and deep (hypodermal) layers of the skin while treating the face. Due to the high density of the focused TriPollar RF, it enables the use of low energy and provides immediate and long-term results.

OXYGENEO Technology is put to use in the Geneo Personal device and provides the benefits of a professional level oxygen facial. The reaction that occurs between the capsules and the gel generates tiny CO2 bubbles on the skin, triggering the Bohr effect, which increases the production of oxygen rich blood to the skin and helps with gentle exfoliation*

Deciding which TriPollar device is right for you is entirely dependent on the needs of your skin and the results you’re after. While both technologies greatly reduce visible signs of aging and leave the skin fully revitalized, each device was created with specific concerns in mind.

STOP X

The STOP X device offers ground-breaking technology and delivers professional results from the comfort of your own home.

It utilizes TriPollar® RF technology to deliver focused radio frequency energy into the skin for an effective treatment that stimulates dermal activity, in turn accelerating the skin’s production of collagen and elastin fibers which help decrease visible signs of aging.

If you’re looking to boost your skin’s collagen level for smoother skin texture and effective antiaging results, the STOP X device is your go-to.

The STOP Vx delivers RF energy into the skin, naturally building collagen. It’s a multi-functional radio frequency device and is enhanced with ELV—a feature that, while the device is in use, delivers vibrations to remind you to keep it in constant motion.

The multi-RF waves run through the dermis layers to ensure an even heating effect on the collagen and elastin layers. This allows for long-lasting results with shorter and faster treatments.

The STOP Vx is right for you if you’re looking to tighten sagging skin while improving the appearance of your skin’s texture through deep cleansing and exfoliation.
